The App does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. We do not collect:

2. Microphone Use

The App uses your device’s microphone solely for in-app functionality (such as game mechanics, sound interaction, or voice-based gameplay elements).

Microphone access is optional and may be revoked in your system settings.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, cloud services, or online APIs. No data is shared with any external party.
